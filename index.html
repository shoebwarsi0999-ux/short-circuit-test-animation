🔬 TEST PROCEDURE

1
Short-circuit the LV (secondary) winding with a thick conductor or shorting bar.
2
Connect Ammeter, Voltmeter, and Wattmeter on the HV (primary) side via a variac.
3
Gradually increase the supply voltage from zero until rated full-load current flows.
4
Record Vsc, Isc, and Wsc at rated current. The Vsc ≈ 5–10% of rated voltage.
5
Calculate Req, Zeq, Xeq, and power factor from measured values. This gives copper loss at full load.
